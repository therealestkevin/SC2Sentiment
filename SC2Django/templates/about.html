This Site

This site uses the game replays graciously uploaded by you users and also scrapes the Starcraft 2 statistics tracking website gggreplays.com to aggregate a comprehensive database of Starcraft matches to encompass the playerbase. All these replays are processed by Blizzard's S2Protocol replay decoder alongside the VADER sentiment analysis tool to ultimately determine Starcraft player attitudes.
